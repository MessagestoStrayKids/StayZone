Stay TALKER-GO Guidelines

Stay TALKER-GO video form

Each section should be roughly 0-2 minutes long


  • Travel (bus, car, plane etc what you doing/where you going/what you looking forward to)

  • hotel (getting in/getting fed/how you feeling?)

  • side quests (while you're out/hanging with friends/going to the pool or gym

  • Concert morning (getting up/how you are feeling/what you doing/what your plans are?)

  • At the venue (what's going on there/what are Stay doing while they wait)

  • Concert (a quick clip of the vibe and what's happening maybe your favorite part?)


No time guidelines - just go for it


  • Back at the hotel/ after (how do you feel now, what did you think, how are you? Anything you want to tell the members)

  • Bonus side quests if you go on them

Stay Vlog form
